Welcome to Dark Moon Rocks.

A lighthouse in the storm.

This is a place for curious minds, wandering souls, night thinkers, early risers, dreamers, makers and misfits who never quite fitted into the neat little boxes the world likes to build.

From a narrowboat on the waterways of Britain comes live radio, podcasts, stories, music, reflections and conversations exploring the strange, the beautiful and the overlooked corners of life.

Some days we follow the sunlight across the water. Other days we sit with the shadows and listen to what they have to teach.

You will find live broadcasts, podcasts, articles, meditations, music and the occasional unexpected rabbit hole waiting quietly in the dark.

So make yourself comfortable.

The kettle is probably on.

The tide is always moving.

And somewhere out there, the lighthouse is still shining.
