Dark Moon Rocks Radio

Dark Moon Rocks Radio is a 24/7 independent audio space combining music, storytelling and psychological insight.

Here you will find ambient and experimental tracks, guided meditations, live radio and reflective content designed to help you slow down, think clearly and reconnect with yourself.

It weaves together original music, hypnotic storytelling, psychological insight and moments of stillness — broadcast from a life lived a little off-grid and slightly sideways to the mainstream.

It is built for listeners who want more than distraction — something thoughtful, immersive and quietly transformative.

Beyond the station, you can access workshops, courses and curated resources designed to support deeper personal exploration.

Welcome to Dark Moon Rocks

In a world increasingly dominated by noise, outrage and distraction, Dark Moon Rocks offers thoughtful conversations, stories, ideas and insights designed to encourage curiosity, reflection and deeper understanding.

Through live radio broadcasts, podcasts, articles, guided meditations, educational resources and digital learning, we explore the myths we live by, the stories we tell ourselves and the hidden patterns that shape our lives.

Founded by Kate Silveness, Dark Moon Rocks combines decades of experience in personal development, hypnosis, coaching, mythology and spiritual exploration with a passion for independent broadcasting and authentic conversation.
